Our Mission

Umoja Foundation is committed to transforming school environments and improving girls' educational outcomes through dignity, education, and proper resources. We believe that every girl deserves the opportunity to learn and thrive in a safe and supportive environment.

Our Vision

Our vision is to create a world where every girl have access to quality education and the necessary resources to succeed. We strive to empower girls to become confident, capable leaders who can make a positive impact in their communities.
